Breakfast

Breakfast Burrito

- Ingredients -

Eggs, Cheese, Salsa, Tortilla, Sausage

- Directions -

Place tortilla on a flat surface and add cooked eggs onto it. After that, put of the cheese and sausage. Place in a microwave for 45 seconds. Finally, put on the salsa and enjoy!

Churro Donuts

- Ingredients -

Dough, Cinnamon, Butter

- Directions -

The first step is to roll up the dough into rings, and place them into an oven for 30 minutes. Once that is done, pour a healthy amount of butter onto each donut. Immediately after you put the butter on, pour cinnamon on each of the donuts and let sit for 5 minutes. Enjoy!

Dessert

Mexican Mocha Bars

- Ingredients -

1 c. all-purpose flour 1 c. unsweetened cocoa 1 tsp. baking powder 1/2 tsp. ground cinnamon 1/2 tsp. salt 3/4 c. butter or margarine 2 c. granulated sugar 5 large eggs 1 tbsp. vanilla extract

- Directions -

Bake, frost, and freeze the bars, tightly wrapped, up to 1 month ahead. Thaw to serve; for neater squares, cut while still cold.

Mexican Chocolate Cake

- Ingredients -

1 box devilâ€™s food cake mix 2 tsp. ground cinnamon 1 c. mini semisweet chocolate chips

- Directions -

Heat oven to 350F. You'll need two 8 x 2-in. round cake pans coated with nonstick spray. Line bottoms with wax paper; coat paper with spray. Prepare cake mix as package directs, adding the cinnamon; stir in chips. Evenly divide batter into prepared pans; bake as package directs. Cool cake layers in pans on wire racks for 15 minutes. Invert; remove pans and wax paper. Cool completely. Frosting: Beat butter in a large bowl with mixer on medium until creamy. On low speed, beat in cocoa. Beat in marshmallow cream, then confectioners' sugar. Increase speed to high and beat until fluffy, about 3 minutes. Place a cake layer on a serving plate. Spread with 3/4 cup frosting; top with remaining cake layer. Frost sides and top of cake.
